About Kurry Box


Home cooked food. Delivered at your doorstep.

KurryBox is a whole new way to eat. Our talented chefs create dishes that get delivered right to your door. It brings to you a unique home dining experience from kitchens of some incredibly talented chefs

Our dishes range from regional tastes to world cuisines and are prepared using fresh ingredients with utmost love and care by chefs who have a passion for food.
